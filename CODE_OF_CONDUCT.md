# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind. Be patient. Assume good faith. This is a hobby project, nobody is getting paid,
so treat people the way you would want to be treated in a friend's garage workshop.

## The slightly longer version

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- being welcoming to newcomers and beginners
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the project

Examples of behaviour that does not fly:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- trolling or deliberately derailing discussions
- publishing other people's private information

## Enforcement

If someone is being a problem, email the maintainer at danny.zweben99@gmail.com. Reports
will be handled discreetly. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, and PRs that
break this code, and may ban repeat offenders.

This is adapted loosely from the Contributor Covenant, kept short on purpose.
